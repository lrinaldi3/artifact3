BREAKING

JOE'S BUY FOUNDER GONE MISSING

Multimillionaire entrepreneur Joe Willis, acting CEO of Joe's Buy, the world's number one e-commerce site, has just announced on Twitter the search for his missing father. Captain Joe Sr. is a retired naval sailor renowned for his efforts in the War of the Sea as well as the founder of Joe's Buy. He was last seen leaving Randy Harbor last Saturday. We are following closely as the events unfold.



ONE WEEK AGO




It was another busy day at Joe's Buy, but something was up.

For the younger Joe, who had just returned from an overseas trip, the meetings went as usual until the buzz of his phone kept bothering him. BZZZ BZZZ BZZZ!

It was his Mother. Joe rarely picks up, especially during meetings. He found out Father had gone off again, no wonder he had so much work! But she seemed very upset. Annoyed, he typed up a letter.

The letter is sent.

Joe gives his secretary the letter to deliver to his mother. As she had said, a mysterious bird was waiting, perched by their home.

Off the bird flew, traveling far out to the sea. That was the end of it... until...
